Tabby Cloud Sync Settings

The easiest way to sync your Tabby settings across devices. (Windows, Mac, Linux)

Browse the plugin documentation ⏱️

Official Plugin?

The plugin is not officially supported by Tabby. It is a community project.
Make with love and share with the community. 💚 You could use it for FREE.

Is it easy to use?

The plugin is easy to use. Just follow the instructions and you are ready to go.

How many services supported?

The plugin supports various services. You can sync your settings with any of popular services.
The new cloud services will be developed in the future. Stay tuned!
